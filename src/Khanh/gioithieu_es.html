ABOUT US

The first modern pharmacy chain in Vietnam

Established on May 2, 2025, Infinity Care has quickly affirmed its pioneering position in the modern pharmaceutical retail industry in Vietnam, with the mission of providing optimal healthcare services and comprehensive experiences for the community.

MISSION

Where customers place their trust and health

Infinity Care strives to provide optimal healthcare services to every customer across Vietnam. Our system has expanded to many major cities and key provinces, with the goal of continuous development to meet the increasing needs of the community.

By the end of 2025, Infinity Care aims to expand its service network with hundreds of facilities nationwide, bringing convenience and the best quality care to the people. At Infinity Care, customers have access to a wide range of high-quality healthcare products. Our team of pharmacists and staff not only possess solid expertise but are also continuously trained to excel in every task assigned, contributing to the trust and love of customers for the Infinity Care brand.
